What cities do you currently operate in?

We currently operate in all major cities across the country, including New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Houston, and Miami. We're continuously expanding to new locations to serve more clients.

Hipcouch Interior Design Service?

Hipcouch Interior Design Service offers a complete package including space planning, furniture selection, color consultation, lighting design, and accessory selection. We handle everything from concept to completion.

What kind of interior designers do you have?

Our team includes specialists in various design styles - from contemporary and minimalist to traditional and eclectic. All our designers are highly qualified with years of experience in creating functional yet beautiful spaces.

Project related questions

How long does it take?

The duration depends on the project scope. Small projects may take 2-4 weeks, while complete home makeovers can take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Can I use my existing furnishings?

Absolutely! We encourage incorporating existing pieces you love. Our designers will assess your current furnishings and suggest how to best integrate them with new elements for a cohesive look.

I put into redesigning my interior?

The investment varies based on project size and complexity. Our services typically range from $2,000 for single room consultations to $15,000+ for full-home designs. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

What do your services cost?

Our services are priced based on the project scope. We offer flat-rate packages for standard services and custom quotes for more complex projects. Detailed pricing is provided after our initial consultation.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer complimentary 30-minute phone consultations to discuss your project and determine if we're a good fit. For in-depth consultations (1 hour), we charge a nominal fee that's credited toward your project if you proceed with us.
